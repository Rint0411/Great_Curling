Heat Wave help you simulate heat air distortion effect easy.
It builds a distortion map in realtime and compose it with original scene color in final pass.
The distortion map render by a particle system in demo scene, however in principle you can render it as any you need.
For example a knife trail, an explosion, etc.

The demo scene demonstrates all features, please refer to it as example usage.
Open it and click play button, Hold left mouse button and move can rotate the camera. Hold W,S,A,D,Q,E key will move the camera.
